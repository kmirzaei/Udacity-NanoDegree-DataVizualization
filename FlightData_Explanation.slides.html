Investigation Overview¶

The overall goal of this presentation is to better understand what factors cause delay or cancellation when you plan a trip.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consist of flight information for all flights within USA in year 2008. It has 7,009,728 rows of data and 29 features/variables
